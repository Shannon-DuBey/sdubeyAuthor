Welcome!

Shannon DuBey is a poet, a painter, a web designer, and now a published author of both Adult/Young Adult Fiction and Children's Stories.


Her back catalogue of writing projects is extensive, and with new projects already in the works there is far more to come from this budding author. Please spend some time browsing the site, getting to know more about the author, and reading excerpts from her published work. When you're ready to finish reading, navigate your way to the "Buy Now" button and own your very own copy in print or e-book.
